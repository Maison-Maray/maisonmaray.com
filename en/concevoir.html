Process · Design— Method 2026

Four moments · One single signature

From the first meeting to the handover of the keys.

— No handovers. No transfers. No surprise variations.

01 First moment

The meeting.

Before the first line is drawn, we meet at your home. We take measurements, note your rituals, list what you wish to keep.

  • Visit of the place and its light
  • Brief on use and style
  • Budget framing
  • Preliminary direction
02 Second moment

The design.

Three weeks later, the project exists, technical plans, atmospheres, photorealistic 3D renders. You walk through the apartment before it exists.

  • Detailed technical plans
  • Moodboards by room
  • Immersive 3D of key rooms
  • Quote fixed on signature
03 Third moment

The site works.

Coordination of craftsmen, weekly visits, photo recap each Friday. No variation without written approval.

  • Coordination of all trades
  • Weekly visit + photo recap
  • Budget contractually locked
  • OPR handover and 1-year guarantee
04 Fourth moment

The furniture.

Bespoke libraries, dressing rooms, kitchens. Tiles, parquet, furniture, selection, orders, delivery.

  • Tiles, parquet, ceramics
  • Bespoke furniture and lighting
  • Textiles, rugs, curtains
  • From the first material to the final candle
