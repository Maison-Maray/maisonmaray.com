Method

Five stages, from the preliminary contract to the rent receipt.

Three non-negotiable criteria: architectural potential, exposure, surface-to-price ratio in relation to the neighbourhood. 80% of the properties presented are off-market.
Technical study: structural condition report, regulatory feasibility (co-ownership, planning), fixed works costing, 36-month rental projection. The go/no-go is documented before signature.
Integrated design, project management and bespoke furniture. In-house handover, OPR, snag-list closure.
Staging for high-end sale or rental: photography, plans, video, coordinated bringing to market. For rentals, qualified tenant dossier within three weeks on average.
Continuous concierge service, annual technical follow-up, quarterly reporting. The property continues to be treated as a project, not as an inert asset.
