Project 35-22.

All facing the Tower.

Location
Paris
Year
2024
Duration
7 months
Budget
On request
The story

One single orientation.

The apartment occupies the twenty-second floor. A raw fact that has dictated everything: every square metre had to face the Eiffel Tower and the Seine, without compromise.

The living area, extended by an open kitchen, embraces the panorama in its entire width. At the rear, four bedrooms: two master suites and two further bedrooms, one of which has its own bathroom. All open onto the same window of Paris.

Living here means learning to count with the light. The grey-blue morning, the copper sunset, the night the Tower paces: hours that become settings. The fittings, restrained, let the view take all the space.
