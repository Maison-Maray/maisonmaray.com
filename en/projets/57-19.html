Project · 57-19

Project 57-19.

Two icons, every day.

Location
Paris
Year
2025
Duration
8 months
Budget
On request
The story

Living between two figures.

The apartment enjoyed a rare double exposure, but the interior layout did nothing with it. Bedrooms huddled where the view invited breathing room; the living area stretched where intimacy was needed. Everything had to move.

The living area was opened to frame the Eiffel Tower and the Seine in their full width. On the night side, the bedrooms shifted to offer, from every pillow, the silhouette of the Statue of Liberty resting on the Île aux Cygnes. The master suite inherited a bathroom and a dressing room worthy of a private guest house.

What is gained here is not a view, but a dialogue. In the morning, the Eiffel reveals itself to the right. In the evening, Liberty stands on the other bank. Between the two, a daily life the décor escorts without ever interrupting.
