Mohamed Maray

My name is Mohamed Maray. I founded Maison Maray because I wanted to design interiors that endure, and that truly resemble those who inhabit them.

Before drawing a single line, I seek to understand how you live. Your hours, your rituals, your objects, what you wish to keep and what we remove. Everything starts there.

I work principally on high-end residential projects. From our first meeting to delivery, I remain your sole contact: design, project management, choice of materials, bespoke furniture, down to the last finish.

What I seek is not an effect; it is a beauty that lasts. Interiors you pass through without tiring of them, that both soothe and inspire, and that grow in value over the long term.

A primary residence, a Parisian pied-à-terre or an investment project: the same exigence each time, the same attention to detail. Based in Paris, I also work across France and abroad, on restrained, architectural, timeless interiors.

Manifesto

Three values held without exception.

01

Architectural sincerity.

Reading a place before transforming it. A high ceiling keeps its scale, never masked. Architecture speaks for itself, without having to disguise itself.

02

Balance of materials.

No material arrives by chance. Each one answers a use, a light, a precise intention. The beauty of an interior owes as much to what it refuses as to what it accepts.

03

Spatial self-evidence.

A successful project is one you don't notice. Not because it's neutral, but because everything is exactly in its place. To the point where you can no longer imagine the space any other way.
